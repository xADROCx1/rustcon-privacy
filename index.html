Privacy Policy for RustCON

Last updated: March 22, 2026

RustCON is a mobile RCON administration tool for Rust game servers, developed by xADROCx. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

RustCON does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data entered into the app including server addresses, RCON passwords, and server profiles is stored locally on your device only.

Network Communication

RustCON communicates directly between your device and your Rust game server via WebSocket RCON. No data passes through any third-party servers operated by the developer.

Data Storage

Server profiles and connection settings are saved locally on your device using standard Android storage. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.

Third Party Services

RustCON uses the Steam Community API to retrieve player avatar images for display purposes only. No user data is sent to Steam beyond the public SteamID required to fetch the avatar image.

Children's Privacy

RustCON is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app is intended for use by Rust game server administrators.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer at the Google Play Store listing for RustCON.
